Description

Abstract / Description:
This dataset shows the position and names of Community Facilities within the State of Queensland.
Community facilities include:
- Art Galleries
- Community Centres
- Court Houses
- Function Centres
- Government House
- Libraries
- Local Government Chambers
- Museums
- Parliament House
- Performing Arts Centres
- Places of Worship
- Post Offices
- Public Halls
- Surf Life Saving Clubs
- Tourist Information Centres
Purpose:
To provide the position and names of Community Facilities for use in Topographic Mapping and in the production of Navigational and Web Based Mapping applications.
Additional Information:
This data has been compiled from numerous sources with attributes within the data describing the source and reliability.
Dource data includes:
- Queensland Digital Cadastral Database
- Queensland Regional Mapping
- Queensland Orthophotography
- Queensland Government Websites
- Satellite Imagery
- Community Websites
- Geoscience Australia

Court Houses, Government House, Local Government Chambers, Parliament House, Post Offices, Surf Life Saving Clubs and Tourist Information Centres are a complete state wide capture. Other feature types within this datset are not complete state wide capture.

History:

Lineage statement:
Features were sourced from government and community websites and the position of the feature is captured from current orthophotography and satellite imagery.

Additional data is supplied by Geoscience Australia as part of their 25K large scale data capture and commitment to the National Topographic Information Coordination Initiative (NTICI). In most cases these features have been identified through fieldwork by Geoscience Australia staff.

Feature Types:
Art Gallery - A facility used as a place set apart for the keeping, exhibition, and study of objects of artistic interest.
Community Centre - A facility where members of a community tend to gather for group activities, social support, public information and other purposes.
Court House - A facility used for holding courts of law and the operation or administration of judicial authorities and commissions.
Function Centre - A large facility used for the holding of conferences and exhibiting manufactured equipment for promotional purposes or for the performance of the visual arts.
Government House - The official complex where the Queensland Governor resides and undertakes duties and administration in relation to the Office of Governor.
Library - A facility used as a place set apart to contain books and other literary material for reading, study and reference.
Local Government Chambers - A facility used for undertaking Local Government duties and administration.
Museum - A facility for the keeping, exhibiting, and study of objects of scientific, artistic, and historical interest.
Parliament House - A facility used by members of the Legislative Assembly to undertake duties and administration.
Performing Arts Centre - A multi-use performance space that is intended for use by various types of the performing arts, including dance, music and theatre.
Place of Worship - A facility used for the activity of public religious worship.
Post Office - A local office of the department for receiving, distributing, and transmitting mail, selling postal stamps, providing telecommunication services
Surf Life Saving Club - A facility located at a patrolled swimming beach and used by members of the local surf lifesaving association for administration, accommodation, the storage of surf lifesaving equipment and social functions.
Tourist Information Centre - A facility used for providing information to persons who travel for pleasure.

Attributes:
FEATURETYPE - Unique feature type that identifies the topographic feature e.g. Court House
NAME - The name of the feature (if available)
ALTERNATENAME - The alternate name of the feature (if available)
FEATURERELIABILITY - Reliability date of the source information
FEATURESOURCE - The imagery, map or data that the spatial location of the feature was obtained from
ATTRIBUTERELIABILITY - Reliability date of the document or authority that the name of the feature was acquired from
ATTRIBUTESOURCE - The source (imagery, map or data) used for attributing (naming) of the feature.
PLANIMETRICACCURACY - The standard deviation of the horizontal positional accuracy.
TEXTNOTE - A comment field.
